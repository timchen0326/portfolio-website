ElectionBuddy — Junior Software Engineer

Author

Tim Chen

Published

August 1, 2025

Overview

This report highlights my contributions and growth as a Junior Software Engineer at ElectionBuddy, where I have been working since August 2025. My work has centered on designing and maintaining workflow automations, supporting cross-functional teams, and improving process reliability across the organization using tools like Zapier, QuickBooks Online, Paperform, and ClickUp.

Role Overview & Key Themes

1. Workflow Automation & Process Improvements

A core part of my role has been automating repetitive and error-prone processes for the Services, Onboarding, Finance, and Admin teams:

  • Built and enhanced Paperform integrations for travel requests, billing workflows, and credit memo generation.
  • Developed and maintained Zapier automations to reduce manual work, optimize task quotas, and improve reliability across pipelines.
  • Addressed logic gaps in billing account creation flows and automated reminders for expiring credits.
  • Streamlined admin automations and improved error handling for edge cases in payment and invoice workflows.

2. Cross-Team Collaboration

I regularly collaborated with stakeholders across multiple teams to iterate quickly and deliver reliable solutions:

  • Worked alongside team members including Chelsea, Ryan, Henrique, and Michael to troubleshoot issues in Pipedrive, QuickBooks Online, and other integrated platforms.
  • Gathered feedback and rapidly iterated on forms and automations in response to team needs.
  • Supported onboarding and offboarding processes, ensuring smooth transitions and handoffs between teams.

3. Technical Troubleshooting & Support

I handled a range of technical challenges requiring both analytical thinking and careful debugging:

  • Debugged Zapier paths, fixed API integration issues, and reconciled legacy vs. non-legacy automations.
  • Investigated and resolved integration issues with Read.ai, QuickBooks Online, and other third-party tools.
  • Supported urgent billing and account issues, ensuring data accuracy across interconnected systems.

4. Documentation & Communication

Clear communication and documentation were essential to keeping stakeholders aligned:

  • Maintained detailed daily and weekly logs summarizing progress, blockers, and next steps.
  • Led live demos for updated forms and automations, clarifying manual vs. automated steps for non-technical team members.
  • Communicated technical limitations and solutions clearly to ensure transparency across teams.

5. Continuous Learning & Growth

I proactively sought out opportunities to grow and contribute beyond my immediate responsibilities:

  • Actively sought feedback and documented pain points for future optimization.
  • Explored new automation ideas including Slack engagement features and team notification workflows.
  • Contributed to brainstorming sessions for improving team efficiency and tool adoption.

Impact Summary

Automation Champion Played a key role in reducing manual work and improving process consistency across multiple teams. Delivered reliable automations that saved time and reduced errors for finance, onboarding, and admin workflows.

Problem Solver Tackled technical and process challenges head-on — debugging integration issues, resolving API inconsistencies, and finding practical solutions in collaboration with colleagues.

Communicator Maintained detailed documentation and provided proactive updates that kept projects on track and ensured all stakeholders remained informed throughout each initiative.

Team Player Responded quickly to teammate feedback, supported colleagues during urgent situations, and contributed to a culture of continuous improvement across the organization.
